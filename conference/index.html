Future Friendly conference series.

Our rolling conference series gathers leaders and change-makers from a broad range of backgrounds to understand how they are redesigning their organisations and services for a better future.

We hosted three conferences throughout 2020:

1st May: Why new world services – what will society need in a post-pandemic world?

28th August: Designing digital public services - how do we switch our services rapidly to digital delivery?

20th November: Designing for financial wellbeing - how do we understand the nuances of money and its effect on culture, community and relationships?
